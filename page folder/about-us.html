About GreenEarth Recycling

Founded in 2017, GreenEarth Recycling is a Cape Town-based company dedicated to waste management and sustainable recycling practices.

Our mission is to raise awareness of environmental issues and provide effective, eco-friendly waste solutions.

We proudly serve residential communities, schools, and businesses across South Africa.
